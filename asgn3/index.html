Transformer Assignment 2.2

 In this part of assignment we have modelled the environment for the trasformer. The environment cosists of
  • Ground : made of multiple squares textured with grass texture
  • Sky and horizon : texture mapped on a cuboid
  • Road : This is made of geometrical multiple geometrical structures including circular arcs and straigt lines with texture mapped
  • Trees : These are made of fractals of multiple lines and at leaf level a texture mapped on triangle
  • Lake and Audience : consisting of texture mapped rectangles

Controls

 Env Keys:

    Key                 | Usage
    ------------------+--------------------------------------------------------
    UP arraow      | Accelerates the car front
    Down arrow    | Deaccelerates the car backward
    Left arrow       | Turn to left
    Right arrow     | Turn to right
    key l                | Switch between two directional lights (Daylight / NightLiht)
    key h               | Toggle the headlight of car (Use it in night. Turn it off gor energy saving in daytime)
    key c               | Change Camera Mode (there are 4 camera modes)
    key b               | Power brake (stops car instantly)
    Enter               | Transform to flying robo
    Escape            | Terminate the Program
